Quality Education is our Motto

Vishwa Adarsha College was established in 2003 AD (2060 BS) as a privately owned academic institute located in Itahari, Sunsari district of Nepal. This College was founded by a group of highly qualified, dedicated and educationists with an express aim to educate and train its learning partners for career-leading to responsible positions of professional in the field of science and Technology, business leadership, government organization and other institution.

It is affiliated to Tribhuvan University and offers Bachelor of Business Studies (BBS) and Master of Business Studies (MBS) programs.
